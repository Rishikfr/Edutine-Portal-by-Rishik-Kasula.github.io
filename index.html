How It Works

Learn about Edutine and what to expect from tutoring sessions.

🎓
What is Edutine?

Edutine is a student-run nonprofit that provides free, one-on-one online tutoring to K-12 students. Our volunteer tutors are high school and college students who are passionate about helping others learn.

📅
How Sessions Work

Once you are matched with a tutor, your tutor will reach out to schedule a weekly session via Google Meet or Zoom. Sessions are typically 45-60 minutes and held once a week. Your tutor will cover the subjects you selected and adjust to your pace.

✅
What to Expect
  • A dedicated volunteer tutor matched to your subjects
  • Flexible scheduling around your calendar
  • Free one-on-one sessions every week
  • Progress updates and homework assignments from your tutor
  • A supportive, friendly learning environment
